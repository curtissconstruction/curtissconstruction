Policy Overview

How inquiry information is collected, used, and protected.

Information We Collect

When a homeowner or client submits a consultation request through Curtiss Construction, we may collect the information they provide directly, including name, phone number, email address, city or project location, project type, budget range, timeline, and project details.

We may also collect limited technical information related to website usage, such as browser type, device information, and general analytics used to improve site performance and user experience.

How We Use Information

The information submitted through our website is used to review project inquiries, respond to consultation requests, communicate about scheduling or scope, and determine whether a project is the right fit for Curtiss Construction.

We may also use submitted contact information to follow up by phone, email, or text message regarding an inquiry that was initiated by the client.

No Sale or Third-Party Marketing

Curtiss Construction does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information may be processed through trusted service providers that support website forms, communications, analytics, or business operations, but only to the extent necessary to deliver those services on our behalf.

Text and Email Communication

By submitting a consultation request, the user consents to receive communication related to their project inquiry. This may include phone calls, emails, or SMS messages directly connected to the request they submitted.

These communications are not promotional mass marketing messages. They are limited to project-related follow-up, coordination, or responses to a request initiated by the user.

Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect the information submitted through our website and business systems. While no system can guarantee absolute security, we use commercially reasonable measures to reduce unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Your Choices

If you would like to update, correct, or request deletion of information previously submitted to Curtiss Construction, contact us directly and we will address the request within a reasonable timeframe, subject to any legal or operational recordkeeping obligations.

Contact

Questions related to this Privacy Policy may be directed to info@curtissconstruction.com.
